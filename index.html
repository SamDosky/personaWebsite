More About Me.

I have always been a deep thinker. Even as a child I can remember myself always trying to process things in as much detail as possible. I loved challenges and solving problems and still do. I think that is why I developed a love and skill for playing chess at a very early age.

By the time I got into my teenage years I had become very interested in data analysis. We didn’t have very good schools for computer science back in my country at that time so I ended up going to engineering school even though my passion was still in data analysis.

I also have a big interest in nutrition science. I used to make data sheets in excel which contained my diet and my daily caloric intake. From that point I began to create excel functions as I learned Visual Basic for Applications. This was probably the beginning of my love for programming.
